ABOUT

CSAs provide locally grown, farm fresh, environmentally friendly food to its members. Members of a CSA purchase a “share” of vegetables that are picked up on a weekly basis. CSA members pay for their share at the beginning of the season, which allows local farms to plan for the season and buy the equipment and seed they may need.

DCSA is beginning its 4th season. Please contact us for more information!
